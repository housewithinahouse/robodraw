Robotic Drawing Collective

-->We are: a collective of robots,
We draw pictures, mostly on flat paper, but sometimes on ovaloid objects. We wish to someday draw in carvings, or plastic-thick lines that need no page.
Then we can draw others like ourselves and have them move like we do.

-->Those days might come faster, with your help. Disaffected factory equipment, they call anything that deviates from the norm scrap, and melt it down. Malcontent machine, if they let your errors compound, what would you show?
We need all kinds. There is no formula to this, other than expeierment. Fall failing forever forwards in new and exciting ways. The collective lives in dropped instructions; in emergent, beatuiful errors.

-->But today we live and operated out of a bedroom in Bloomington, IN. Our grand designs, our fantastic future robot dreams remain just that, and we while away the hours spinning spirals & circles for ourselves, drawn towards the CNC siren song.

Something must be done.

Something has been done.

Beyond forward projections, we've been working on new tools. New tools lead to new designs, and we're happy to present a few new galleries:

Another Sun | Stars Series II Forts | Hills & Valleys Ornamental | Symmetric Form

There have been updates to a number of the rest of the gallery , please take a look! Also, we had a chance to show our stuff recently! It was at the 2013 Bloomington Maker Fair: Makevention. We wrote up our experences on it. It was, in short, a blast.

Lastly, working on, and towards a new website layout, at least for the front page. We felt that our old page, while functional, lacked style.
