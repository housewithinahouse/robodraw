Circles | Itterated Forms
— 2012 —

These nested circles were made to test calibration on our newest family member, the Mimaki CG-45. It was having trouble with losing registration, and these circles were a way of troubleshooting. A quick opening up, and the problem was solved.

Circles have a very simple geometry, and ever after calibration, we've continued to make more of pages of circles, repeating and transfoming them around the page.

More circles someday!

These were designed using Inkscape + Python,
and executed by a modified Mimaki CG-45 Vinyl Cutter

These are drawn with a Uniball Deluxe Fine
on 80lb Sawgrass Cardstock.
