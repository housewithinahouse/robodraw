Testing | First Trials
— 2012 —

These are the first prints yielded by our Mimaki CG-45. This robot was a 1989 vinyl cutter I got at a garage sale.

A little tinkering was necessary to get it working. The tool holder now holds pens/markers/other writing utensils. It's still a bit finicky.

These were designed using Inkscape + Python,
and executed by a modified Mimaki CG-45 Vinyl Cutter

These were drawn with a Uniball Deluxe Fine that I found in the trash
on 80lb Sawgrass Cardstock.
