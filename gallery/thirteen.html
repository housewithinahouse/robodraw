New Works | Unsorted Series
— 2013 —

After our Fell Building Show, the first exhibition, drawing style began to stagnate. To defeat this, the stale forms & increasingly familier tools were cast aside, favoring instead the trial, the unrepeatable work informed from happenstance. Forward towards new experments.

A few of the thrusts to drawings produced this year:
- Quadralateral & Bilateral Symetry
- Succesively Itterated Shapes
- Markers+Traced Strokes
- Textual Manipulation
- Software Self Abuse

The source of the cardstock we had been useing ran dry, so a lot of these are just on plain paper, scavanged stock, or random junk. Our pens ran dry, so we grabbed whatever we could find.

These were designed using Inkscape + Python,
and executed by a modified Mimaki CG-45 Vinyl Cutter

Draw with whatever we crammed into our tool holder including:
and a wide selection of Moonglow and Metallic Sakura Gelly-Roll Pens
sharpies, pens, pencils, markers, cutters, whiteout, whatever
draw on whatever stock was at hand.
